## Genetic basis of virulence and persistence
We use forward genetics and experimental crosses to identify parasite loci that control virulence, persistence, and drug susceptibility in *Cryptosporidium*.

## Forward genetics and genome engineering
Our lab develops and applies scalable genetic tools, including experimental crosses, bulk segregant analysis, and genome engineering approaches, to dissect complex parasite traits.
